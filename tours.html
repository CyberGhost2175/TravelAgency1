Tours from Astana

Astana -> Turkey

Turkey is a country of vibrant culture and ancient civilizations. The relaxing atmosphere of a beach holiday, lively nightlife, friendliness and hospitality of the local population, ancient history and developed infrastructure of the resorts are wonderfully combined here.

Astana -> Egypt

Egypt is a country with a long and fascinating history. Most of its territory is covered with desert, all life takes place on the shores of the great Nile, the coasts of the Mediterranean and the Red Seas. The hot climate, warm sea, ancient monuments, developed tourist infrastructure are the key to Egypt's popularity.

Astana -> UAE

The United Arab Emirates is a state on the Arabian Peninsula. A rich country with amazing sights, luxury hotels, the highest skyscrapers, snow-white beaches. Travelers come here for a rich vacation, oriental flavor and high-class service.
