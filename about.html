How to book a tour

  1. Choose a Tour: Browse our offers on the website or contact our managers via phone, WhatsApp, or social media to get recommendations and select the perfect tour.
  2. Consult with a Manager: Our experienced specialists will answer all your questions, help clarify trip details, accommodation options, flights, and suggest additional excursions and services.
  3. Preliminary Booking: After choosing a tour, you can make a preliminary booking. You’ll need to provide your contact information, passport details for flight bookings, and your preferred payment method.
  4. Booking Confirmation and Payment: Once all details are agreed upon, you will receive an invoice for payment. We offer several convenient payment options: credit card, bank transfer, or cash at our office.
  5. Receive Your Documents: After payment is confirmed, you will receive all necessary documents: electronic flight tickets, hotel vouchers, transfers, and excursion details, along with a detailed trip itinerary.
  6. Prepare for Your Trip: We will send you a reminder with important information, including visa requirements, entry rules, weather conditions, and helpful tips for your comfortable trip.
  7. Enjoy Your Journey: Everything is set! All that’s left is to enjoy your trip, and we’ll always be available to assist with any questions.
